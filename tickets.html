Tickets

All-inclusive options

As we have exclusive-use of the hotel, only Úll attendees can stay in the hotel for the 2 conference nights. Rooms are exclusively available through us for those two nights.

We can accommodate double rooms, single rooms and twin rooms for those who wish to share.

Úll also has a children’s track, and the hotel has family rooms and conjoining rooms. If you have any questions simply get in touch and we’ll accommodate you.

Eagrán

The Eagrán ticket is our supporters ticket. It consists of 4 nights accommodation at the hotel, admission to the conference as well as admission to pre- and post- conference events.

Stay 2 Nights

For those of you who want to get-in-get out you can enjoy the full conference experience by staying 2 nights at the Europe.
